Therefore, we assure you that we do not collect or store any user information. It is akin to enjoying the breathtaking scenery during your travels without any concerns about personal information leakage.

Information Collection and Usage:

We do not require or request any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. As a result, you can browse and use our DNS query website anonymously, without the need to provide any personal details.

Log Data:

We may collect certain non-personally identifiable information automatically when you access our website. This data, known as log data, includes your IP address, browser type, operating system, the pages you visit, the time and date of your visit, and other anonymous usage statistics. However, please note that this information is solely used for analytical purposes to improve the performance and functionality of our website. It is not linked to any personally identifiable information and is not used to track your online activities.

Cookies:

Our website does not use cookies to collect any personal information. However, we may use session cookies to enhance your browsing experience. These temporary cookies are stored on your device's memory only during your session and are automatically deleted when you close your browser.

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
